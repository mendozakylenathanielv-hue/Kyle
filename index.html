How AI Is Being Used as an Assistant in Coding

March 16, 2026

Artificial intelligence is a prevalent technology today. It is used to generate essays, images, videos, and even code. As technology advances, AI has become a powerful tool that helps people complete tasks more efficiently. In this blog, we will discuss how AI is used in coding. Many students, developers, and programmers use AI to assist them in solving problems and improving productivity.

AI helps programmers with coding through autocomplete, creating small snippets such as functions to complete code, finding bugs, and other similar uses. This makes work more efficient because finding bugs can sometimes be difficult, and scanning through entire codebases can take a long time. With the use of AI, however, this process can be done in just a few minutes, and it can even suggest fixes for the bug.
